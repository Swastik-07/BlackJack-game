Rules:

  • All blackjack rules.
  • Game is played with infinite deck.
  • Maximum 5 cards can be drawn.
  • If you draw 5 cards without hitting 21 you win.
  • If you or PC get a joker before hitting 21 you win.
  • PC has to keep drawing cards until it's score is less than 17.
  • 777 is a Jackpot!
